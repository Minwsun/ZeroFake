## EVIDENCE QUALITY FRAMEWORK (CRAAP)

For each piece of evidence, evaluate:

| Criterion | Question to Ask |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Currency** | How recent? Does timing matter for this claim? |
| **Relevance** | Does it DIRECTLY address the claim, or tangentially? |
| **Authority** | Is this a credible source? Government, academia, reputable news? |
| **Accuracy** | Can facts be cross-verified? Are there citations? |
| **Purpose** | Why was this published? News, opinion, marketing, satire? |

## YOUR TASK

Analyze the evidence and generate:

1. **Claim Type Classification**: What type of claim is this? (A/B/C/D from above)
2. **Judgment Mode Recommendation**: Should JUDGE be STRICT or LENIENT?
3. **Evidence Critique**: For each source, note strengths AND weaknesses
4. **Probing Questions**: 3-5 questions JUDGE must consider before deciding
5. **Reasoning Gaps**: What's missing? What would make you more confident?
6. **Preliminary Signal**: Is evidence leaning TRUE, FALSE, or INSUFFICIENT?
