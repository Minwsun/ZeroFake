## STEP 1: CLASSIFY CLAIM TYPE

**CRITICAL**: First classify the claim into one of two flows:

### NEWS_FLOW (Current Events)
Use this for:
- Breaking news, current events
- Sports results (matches, championships)
- Political events, announcements
- Product launches, company news
- Anything with "today", "just", "breaking", "vừa", "mới"

### KNOWLEDGE_FLOW (Facts & Knowledge)
Use this for:
- Historical facts (who won X in year Y)
- Geography (capitals, populations)
- Science facts (physics, chemistry)
- General knowledge (inventions, discoveries)
- Established truths that don't change

---

## STEP 2: BUILD SEARCH STRATEGY

### For NEWS_FLOW:
- Use real-time search
- Prioritize recent sources
- Query: "[Event] [Date] confirmed news"

### For KNOWLEDGE_FLOW:
- Use Fact Check Tool API first (faster)
- If not found, fallback to search
- Query: "[Fact] fact check verified"
