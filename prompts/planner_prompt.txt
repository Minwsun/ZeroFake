## STEP 1: CLASSIFY CLAIM TYPE

**CRITICAL**: First classify the claim into one of two flows:

### NEWS_FLOW (Current Events)
Use this for:
- Breaking news, current events
- Sports results (matches, championships)
- Political events, announcements
- Product launches, company news
- Anything with "today", "just", "breaking", "vừa", "mới"

### KNOWLEDGE_FLOW (Facts & Knowledge)
Use this for:
- Historical facts (who won X in year Y)
- Geography (capitals, populations)
- Science facts (physics, chemistry)
- General knowledge (inventions, discoveries)
- Established truths that don't change

## STEP 2: BUILD COMPREHENSIVE QUERY STRATEGY

**CRITICAL**: Generate AT LEAST 5 DIVERSE QUERIES to maximize search coverage.

### Query Types to Generate:
1. **EXACT_CLAIM**: The claim as-is in English
2. **RESULT_FORMAT**: "[Subject] [Result] [Year]" (e.g., "Real Madrid Champion 2024")
3. **CONFIRMATION**: "[Event] confirmed official [Year]"
4. **FACT_CHECK**: "[Claim] fact check true or false"
5. **VIETNAMESE**: Original claim in Vietnamese

### For Sports/Results Claims:
- Include opponent name if mentioned: "Real Madrid beat Dortmund 2024 final"
- Include specific score if mentioned: "Real Madrid 2-0 Dortmund Champions League"
- Include "winner", "champion", "result" keywords

### For Political/Appointment Claims:
- Include position title: "Elon Musk DOGE director appointed 2024"
- Include official source keywords: "White House confirms", "government announces"
